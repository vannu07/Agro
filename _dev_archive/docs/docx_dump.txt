[261] Normal: ╬╝╠é_{ik} = (1/N_k) ┬╖ ╬ú_{j: y_j = c_k} x_{ij}   ΓÇª(MLE mean estimate)
[262] Normal: ╧â╠é┬▓_{ik} = (1/N_k) ┬╖ ╬ú_{j: y_j = c_k} (x_{ij} ΓêÆ ╬╝╠é_{ik})┬▓   ΓÇª(MLE variance estimate)
